Acumulus extension events
=========================

The Acumulus extension defines 3 events:

1) acumulus_invoice_created
2) acumulus_invoice_send_before
2) acumulus_invoice_send_after

See the example extension AcumulusCustomiseInvoice for usage and documentation.
You can download this example extension from the Acumulus forum at
https://forum.acumulus.nl/index.php/topic,5590.0.html.
